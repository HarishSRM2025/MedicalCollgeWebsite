ADMISSION PROCESS

Transparent and merit-based admission through centralized counseling

Eligibility Criteria

NEET-UG qualified for MBBS, NEET-PG for postgraduate programs. Age limits and qualifying marks as per NMC guidelines apply.

Counseling Registration

Register for state or management quota counseling. Fill choice of colleges and courses based on NEET rank and preferences.

Document Verification

Attend document verification with original certificates including NEET scorecard, academic records, and identity proofs.

Seat Allotment

Seats allotted based on merit, rank, and choice filling. Multiple rounds of counseling conducted until all seats filled.

Fee Payment

Pay admission fees within stipulated time. Fee structure as per government regulations with transparent breakup provided.

Admission Confirmation

Complete admission formalities, medical examination, and orientation program before commencement of classes.
