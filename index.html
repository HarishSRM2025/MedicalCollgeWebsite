Mission & Vision

Commitment To Medical Departments In Rendering Excellent Care, Research, And Education

Our Vision

To serve the community as a center of excellence through creating innovative, collaborative solutions along with life-long learning, and serve as a world-class health care provider with exceptional services.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive medical education that transforms learners into leaders by equipping every student in an array of medical specialties, with a distinct commitment to serving the needs of rural communities and underserved populations.
